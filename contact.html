ALESSANDRO BUFFI

Alessandro Buffi is an Italian architect working between Perugia and Florence — trained in architecture and building engineering, with professional experience between Italy, New York and London, and a long collaboration with the Florentine studio of architect Francesco Buffi on projects across Tuscany. The studio follows a small number of commissions at a time, each led personally from the first walk on the land to the direction of works.

Your enquiry is read and answered personally by the architect — not by an office manager. First conversations are free of charge and without obligation.
