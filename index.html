Approach — architecture of position

Facing the historic presence of Assisi, architecture does not seek centrality. It seeks position.

Every site in central Italy already belongs to a composition — a ridge of olive trees, the fall of a valley, the silhouette of a basilica. Architecture of position means measuring every wall, opening and terrace against topography, landscape and the historic city. It is the method behind Alma, and behind every commission the studio takes on.
